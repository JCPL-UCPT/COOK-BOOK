SISIG

WHAT YOU NEED:

  1. 1 ½ lbs. Pork belly
  2. 3 tablespoons Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  3. 1 lb. pig face maskara
  4. 4 ounces chicken liver
  5. 2 pieces onion
  6. ½ cup chicharon crumbled
  7. 3 tablespoons Lady’s Choice Mayonnaise
  8. 2 teaspoons onion powder
  9. ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  10. ½ teaspoon chili flakes (optional)
  11. 1 tablespoon butter
  12. 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  13. 2 quarts water

HOW TO MAKE IT:

  1. Boil water in a pot. Add pork belly and pig face. Cover and continue to boil for 1 hour in medium heat. Drain water and let the meat cool down.
    1 ½ lbs. Pork belly, 2 quarts water, 1 lb. pig face
  2. Grill boiled meats for 5 minutes per side. Remove from the grill and chop into small pieces. Note: feel free to mince the meat if preferred.
  3. Heat oil on a pan. Sauté onion for 1 minute.
    2 pieces onion, 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  4. Add chicken liver. Continue to sauté until thoroughly cooked. Mash the liver while cooking until it breaks down into small pieces.
    4 ounces chicken liver
  5. Add minced meat and chicharon into the pan. Stir until all ingredients are evenly distributed.
    ½ cup chicharon
  6. Add chili flakes, onion powder, and ground black pepper.
    ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper, ½ teaspoon chili flakes, 2 teaspoons onion powder
  7. Pour Knorr Liquid Seasoning. Stir.
    3 tablespoons Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  8. Add Lady’s Choice Mayonnaise. Stir and cook for 3 minutes. Set aside.
    3 tablespoons Lady’s Choice Mayonnaise
  9. Heat a metal plate. Add butter. Transfer cooked sisig onto the metal plate. Continue to cook until it sizzles. Top with more chicharon.
    1 tablespoon butter
  10. Serve with calamansi or lime. Serve. Share and enjoy with white rice.
