BULALO

WHAT YOU NEED:

  1. 2 lbs beef shank
  2. ½ cabbage whole leaf individually detached
  3. 1 bundle bok choy
  4. 2 cobs corn each cut into 3 parts
  5. 2 Tablespoons whole peppercorn
  6. ½ cup green onions cut into 1 ½ inch pieces
  7. 1 onion
  8. 34 ounces water
  9. Fish sauce to taste (optional)

HOW TO MAKE IT:

  1. In a big cooking pot, pour in water and bring to a boil.
  2. Put in the beef shank followed by the onion and whole peppercorn, then simmer for 1.5 hours (30 minutes if using a pressure cooker) or until the meat is tender.
  3. Add the corn and simmer for another 10 minutes.
  4. Add the fish sauce, cabbage, pechay (bok choy), and green onion (onion leeks).
  5. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!
