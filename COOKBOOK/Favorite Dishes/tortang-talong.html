TORTANG TALONG

WHAT YOU NEED:

  1. 4 pieces Chinese eggplant
  2. 2 pieces raw eggs
  3. 1 teaspoon salt
  4. 6 tablespoons cooking oil

HOW TO MAKE IT:

  1. Grill the eggplant until the color of the skin turns almost black.
  2. Let the eggplant cool for a while, then peel off the skin. Set aside.
  3. Crack the eggs and place them in a bowl.
  4. Add salt and beat the eggs.
  5. Place the eggplant on a flat surface and flatten using a fork.
  6. Dip the flattened eggplant in the beaten egg mixture.
  7. Heat the pan and pour in the cooking oil.
  8. Fry the eggplant (that was dipped in the beaten mixture). Make sure that both sides are cooked. Frying time will take about 3 to 4 minutes per side on medium heat.
