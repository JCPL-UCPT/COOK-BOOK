DRAGON BEEF

WHAT YOU NEED:

  1. BEEF: 500 GMS
  2. Onion: 1, sliced
  3. Capsicum: 1/2 sliced lengthwise
  4. Green chilies: 2, slit
  5. Garlic: 8 cloves, sliced
  6. Ginger: 1 inch, sliced
  7. Whole Kashmiri Chilies: 3-4
  8. Pepper powder: 1 tbsp
  9. Crushed Kashmiri chilies: 1 tbsp
  10. Cashew nuts: 1 tbsp
  11. Spring onions: 2-3 sprigs
  12. Soy sauce: 1 tbsp + 1 tbsp
  13. Vinegar: 1 tsp
  14. Salt: 3/4 to 1 tsp
  15. Cornflour: 2 tbsp
  16. Flour: 1 tbsp
  17. Red chilli sauce: 1 tsp + 2 tbsp
  18. Sugar: A fat pinch
  19. Oil: 2-3 tbsp

HOW TO MAKE IT:

  1. Pressure cook the beef with 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tsp vinegar, 3/4 tsp salt for 20 mins.
  2. Once the meat is cooked and if there is any stock left, dry it up and then transfer it to a bowl.
  3. Marinate the meat with 1 tsp red chili sauce, cornflour, and flour. Mix this very well.
  4. Heat a kadai or wok with oil and add half of the marinated beef and fry and then keep aside. Repeat the same with the other half.
  5. Into the same oil, add in whole Kashmiri chilies (broken), cashew nuts, garlic, and ginger. Fry this lightly.
  6. Add in onion, capsicum, green chilies, and little salt. Stir fry it on high flame for 1 min. Add in pepper powder and crushed red chili flakes, mix well.
  7. Add in 2 tbsp chili sauce and 1 tbsp soy sauce, mix well.
  8. Once everything is nicely mixed, add in the fried beef and mix well. Add in a fat pinch of sugar.
  9. Lastly sprinkle spring onions.
  10. Serve with fried rice or as an appetizer.
